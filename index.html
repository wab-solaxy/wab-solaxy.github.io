Introduction to Solaxy

Solaxy​ is an emerging Layer 2 scaling solution built on the Solana blockchain, aiming to enhance transaction speed, scalability, and reliability. By integrating innovative rollup architecture, Solaxy seeks to address existing challenges within the Solana network, such as congestion and failed transactions, thereby optimizing its core strengths of speed and low fees.

Key Features of Solaxy

  • Layer 2 Solution: Solaxy is the first Layer 2 platform for Solana, utilizing rollup technology to improve scalability and transaction throughput. ​

  • $SOLX Token: The native cryptocurrency of the Solaxy ecosystem, $SOLX, serves as the primary medium for transaction fees and network operations. ​

  • Multi-Chain Compatibility: $SOLX supports both Ethereum and Solana networks, facilitating interoperability and broadening its utility across multiple blockchain ecosystems. ​
